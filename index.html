Tips

  1. Always keep the system volume lower than 25%. Volume can be very unreliable before you get used to knowing a patch, so approach new patches with caution. You can always turn volume up, but if you blow your headphones or even worse, your ears, you can't reverse that...
  2. It's fun to experiemnt and break things, that's what Pure Data is all about.
  3. Purr Data allows you to customise the look of your patches so you're not facing a glaring white page for hours on end...
